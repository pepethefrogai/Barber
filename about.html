About Northline

Classic standards, modern barbering.

We focus on sharp cuts, a clean atmosphere, and a professional experience that keeps customers coming back.

Our Story

Northline Barbers was built around one simple idea, keep things sharp, consistent, and easy. Whether it’s a regular trim, a skin fade, or a full haircut and beard appointment, the goal is always the same, quality work and a smooth customer experience.


We combine the feel of a trusted local barbershop with a more premium, modern standard. Clean service, attention to detail, and no unnecessary fuss.

Why Choose Us

  • Skilled barbers with a clean, consistent finish
  • Easy booking and straightforward service
  • Modern shop feel with a strong local reputation
  • Walk-ins welcome alongside booked appointments
